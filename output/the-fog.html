The Fog

Mon 01 February 2016

In not so far future cybernetic fog descends upon the world in a form of billions of agents. By measuring, controlling, coordinating and transacting with each other they create invisible symbiotic mesh of peer-to-peer (P2P) machine-to-machine (M2M) communications hidden underneath the human usable applications. Services and applications gravitates from data black hole centralized silos to the edges of the Network, where those entities live. Utilizing blockchain technology for coordinating, negotiating and value transferring between nodes, with the smart contracts enabling algorithmification and codefication of relationships between humans, as well as autonomous organizations, new paradigms emerges and new socio-economic-civilization structure is created. A.I. driven agents dwelling on blockchains acts autonomously with their own agenda or as digital assistants and representatives of humans in this computing mesh world. Every device becomes a node. "Fog computing" and "Distributed Autonomous Organization" becomes mainstream buzzwords.
